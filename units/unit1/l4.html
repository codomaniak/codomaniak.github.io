Grammar

Verb to be - negative & questions

With the verb to be, change the position of the subject and the verb to make a question.

You are a student.

Are you a student?

You are not (aren't) a student.

In English there are two kinds of questions:

Wh- questions - questions with a question word

Where are you from?

Yes/no questions - questions with no question word

Are you a student?

Yes/no questions have a short answer.

Yes, I'am. No, I'm not.
