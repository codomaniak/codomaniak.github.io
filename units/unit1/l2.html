Grammar

Plurals

  • Add s: a doll + s = two dolls
  • a toy - two toys
  • Add es (For most nouns that end in ch, sh, s, ss, x, z): bus - buses, class — classes, toothbrush - toothbrushes, match – matches, fox - foxes
  • Add ies: a lady - two ladies
  • Add ves (For most nouns that end in f or fe): life - lives, scarf – scarves

Exceptions

  • a child — children
  • a foot - feet
  • a tooth - teeth
  • a woman - women
  • a man - men
  • a mouse - mice
