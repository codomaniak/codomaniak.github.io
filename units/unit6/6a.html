Grammar: past simple (irregular verbs)

Irregular verbs have a different form in the pasl simple affirmative.

  • be - was
  • eat -ate
  • go - went
  • make - made
  • see - saw
  • have - had

Look at the blogs. Find twelve different irregular past tense affirmative verbs. Write the past form and the infinitive.
