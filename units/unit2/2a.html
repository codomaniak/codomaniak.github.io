Present simple

  • I live in Russia.
  • She lives in China.
  • Do you like animals?
  • Does he play football?
  • I don't like snakes.
  • My cat doesn't eat fish.

Spelling rules

  • verb + s

    I eat - he eats, I like - he likes

  • verb -ss/-sh/-ch/-x/-o + es

    I go - he goes, I wash - she washes

  • verb ending in a consonant y: -ies

    I cry - he cries but I say - he says

Reading rules

  • [f], [k], [p], [t] - [s]

    laughs

  • [s], [ʃ], [tʃ], [dƷ], [z] – [iz]

    misses

  • other sounds - [z] plays

    plays
