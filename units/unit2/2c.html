Wh- questions

What, where, when, who, why and how are question words.

Put them at the beginning of the question.

How are you?

Where are you from?

What is his name?

Who does he live with?

Why does he live at home?

Possessive 's

Use 's to show possession.

John's cousin

my son's bedroom

If the word ends in an -s, add ‘

His parents' house.

The babies' rooms.

Rearrange the words to make questions.

live do where you ?

with who live you do ?

their what names are ?

old they how are ?
