Reading

Read the letter

Dear Bob and Madge,

Welcome to my home. I hope you have a nice stay for the weeks you are here. Here is some useful information about the house and the town. Crawford is a nice small town. There are some good shops but there isn't a cinema or theatre. There is a beautiful park near to the river. The river is about 15 minutes from my house. There is a small shop on the street. My house, number 15, is opposite the shop and next to a school. Don't worry, in the holiday the school is closed so it is quiet.

OK, inside the house. There are three rooms on the ground floor the kitchen, a living room and a small room at the back with the washing machine. There are some plants in the kitchen next to the fridge. Please water them. The TV is on the table next to the bookcase in the living room. There are two bedrooms on the first floor. Please use the one opposite the bathroom. In the study - next to the bathroom - there is a computer and a small stereo with my CDs.

So, enjoy your stay.

Malcolm

Answer the questions

  1. Who is it to?
  2. Who is it from?
  3. What is it about?

Decide if the sentences are true or false

  • There is a beautiful park near the house.
  • Malcolm's house is next to the shop.
  • There is a table in the living room.
  • The washing machine is in the kitchen.
  • There are some plants on the fridge.
  • There are three bedrooms in the house.
  • There is a computer in the bedroom.
  • The stereo and CDs are in the study.

Language focus

Complete the sentences with is or has

  • My father's name ___ Andy.
  • He ___ fat.
  • He ___ grey hair.
  • My mother ___ short.
  • She ___ brown eyes.
  • My brother ___ handsome.
  • He ___ a pretty wife.
  • She ___ glasses.

Choose the correct words to make these sentences true for your family.

  • My father is average height / short / tall.
  • He has brown /blue / green eyes.
  • He has blond / black / no hair.
  • He is old / middle-aged /young.
  • My brother /sister is average height / short / tall.
  • He /She is handsome / beautiful / pretty / average-looking.
  • He / She is thin / fat.
