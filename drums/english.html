🥁 Specialised Branch · Orchestral / Latin / World

Multi‑percussionist competencies beyond the drumset

Mallet Keyboard

Vibes, marimba, xylo — 4‑mallet independence, dampening, interval skipping

Timpani

Tonal accuracy (pedaling), stroke types (legato, staccato, portato), muffling

Accessories

Triangle damping, tambourine thumb roll, cymbal selection, conga/bongo hand technique

World Rhythm Vocabulary

Samba Batucada, West African Djembe (bass/slap/tone), Darbuka, orchestral excerpts

📌 How to use this map

  • Self‑assessment — highlight the box you currently occupy in each competency
  • Goal setting — pick one competency to move from intermediate → advanced each month
  • Band audition — prioritise Musicality & Professional domains; a reliable Novice often beats a difficult Expert
